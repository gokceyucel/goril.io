Play Rush Burger

Make your hamburgers ready for your customers. Try to be as quick as you can for high score.

Everyone is hungry here

Random receipts will start falling. Difficulty of receipts will vary according to your score. You will get +1 chef hat in every correct receipt after score of 20.

Order of ingredients

Be careful! Order of the ingredients can be tricky. You can't know the customer.

Correct burger

You will see the happy customer and get +1 when your hamburger is correct and ready to eat.

Different time for different receipts

Every receipt will have a different ready time. Watch out the tower burger :)

Don't panic

Panic will destroy your concentration and you will start losing all your chances. Avoid it!

Animal friendly game

Your trashed ingredients will go to the animals in need. With every 1000 downloads of the game, we donate 50kg of food to our friends.

Play on multiple device

You can play the game on multiple platforms with the power of Unity. We will do our best to deliver hamburgers on more platforms other than IOS and Android.
